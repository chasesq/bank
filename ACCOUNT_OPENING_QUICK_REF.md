## How to Use It

### Step 1: Click "Open a new account"
From the login page footer, click the "Open a new account" button.

### Step 2: Choose Account Type
- **Checking Account** - $0 deposit, unlimited transactions, no fees
- **Savings Account** - Earn 4.25% APY on your savings
- **Money Market** - $2,500 minimum, 5.00% APY, check writing

### Step 3: Enter Account Details
- **Account Name** (required) - e.g., "My Checking", "Emergency Fund"
- **Initial Deposit** (optional) - Any amount, $0 minimum
- **Funding Source** - Existing account, debit card, wire, or skip
- **Accept Terms** - Check the terms & conditions box

### Step 4: Click "Open Account Instantly"
That's it! Your account is created in real-time.

### Step 5: See Confirmation
Account number, routing number, and balance displayed immediately.

## Troubleshooting

### Form Won't Submit
**Solution**: Make sure account name is entered and terms are accepted

### Account Name Blank
**Solution**: Enter a name like "My Checking" or "Savings"

### Deposit Rejected
**Solution**: 
- Check amount is valid number
- Funding source may have limits
- Try different source or smaller amount

### No Confirmation
**Solution**:
- Check internet connection
- Look for SMS/email
- Check in-app notifications
- Verify account in dashboard
