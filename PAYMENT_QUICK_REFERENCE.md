## 📋 The 4 Main Payment Options

### 1️⃣ Send Money with Zelle® 💳
**How**: Pay & Transfer → Send money with Zelle®
- **What**: Fast & free transfers to Zelle-enabled recipients
- **Time**: Instant processing
- **Fee**: None
- **Flow**: Select contact → Amount → Send

### 2️⃣ Transfer Between Accounts 🔄
**How**: Pay & Transfer → Transfer
- **What**: Move money between your own accounts
- **Time**: Immediate
- **Fee**: None
- **Flow**: From account → To account → Amount → Transfer

### 3️⃣ Pay Bills 📋
**How**: Pay & Transfer → Pay bills
- **What**: Pay utility, insurance, credit card, subscription bills
- **Time**: Scheduled or immediate
- **Fee**: None
- **Flow**: Select payee → Amount → Date → Schedule/Pay

### 4️⃣ Wires & Global Transfers 🌍
**How**: Pay & Transfer → Wires & global
- **What**: Domestic and international wire transfers
- **Time**: 1-3 business days
- **Fee**: Varies by wire type
- **Flow**: Enter details → Verify codes → Process → Confirm

## 🎯 Quick Start

1. **Open App** → See accounts & balance
2. **Tap Pay & Transfer** → See 4 payment options
3. **Choose Option** → Spinner appears
4. **Wait ~200ms** → Drawer opens with data
5. **Complete Transaction** → See balance update instantly
6. **Check History** → New transaction appears immediately

## 🚨 What If...

**Spinner doesn't appear?**
→ Try clicking again, may be loading

**Drawer opens slowly?**
→ First load preloads data, subsequent opens instant

**Data doesn't update?**
→ Check internet connection, balances refresh automatically

**Error on submit?**
→ Fix validation errors, form data persists, try again

**On mobile, drawer is too small?**
→ Rotate phone or tap full-screen icon

## 🆘 Common Issues & Fixes

|-------|-----|
| Button won't respond | Wait for spinner to finish, try again |

| Balance not updating | Refresh page or close/reopen drawer |

| Mobile keyboard blocking fields | Scroll form up or use landscape mode |
